Q: How can I add RSS feeds?

A: Navigate to the RSS section in the app. You can add feeds by entering a website URL - the app will automatically discover available RSS feeds from that site.

Q: How do I change the app language?

A: The app automatically detects your device's language setting. To change the language, modify your device's language settings in iOS system preferences.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?

A: If you've previously purchased a subscription, you can restore it by going to Settings in the app and tapping "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
