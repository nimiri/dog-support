About Dog App

Dog App is a virtual dog care application that combines health tracking, feeding interactions, weather information, and RSS feed reading. Take care of your virtual dog while tracking your real-world health activities through Apple HealthKit integration.

Key Features

  • Virtual Dog Care - Feed and interact with your virtual dog
  • Health Tracking - Apple HealthKit integration for activity monitoring
  • Weather Information - Real-time weather updates using Apple WeatherKit
  • RSS Feed Reader - Stay updated with your favorite news sources
  • Multi-language Support - Available in 7 languages (Japanese, English, German, Spanish, French, Korean, Chinese)

System Requirements

  • iOS: iOS 13.4 or later
  • Android: Android 5.0 or later
  • Optional: Apple HealthKit for health tracking features
  • Optional: Location permissions for weather features

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I enable weather features?

A: To use weather features, you need to grant location permissions:

  • Go to iOS Settings app
  • Navigate to "Privacy & Security" ‚Üí "Location Services"
  • Find "Dog App" and allow location access
Q: How do I enable health tracking?

A: Health tracking requires Apple HealthKit permissions:

  • When prompted by the app, tap "Allow" to grant HealthKit access
  • You can modify permissions later in iOS Settings ‚Üí Health ‚Üí Data Access & Devices
Q: How does the feeding system work?

A: You can select food items to feed your virtual dog. The dog will react to the food, and you can observe its eating animation. Regular feeding keeps your dog happy!

Q: What is the subscription model?

A: The app offers premium features through a subscription model managed by RevenueCat. You can try premium features during a trial period. After the trial, you'll need an active subscription to continue using premium features.

Q: How can I add RSS feeds?

A: Navigate to the RSS section in the app. You can add feeds by entering a website URL - the app will automatically discover available RSS feeds from that site.

Q: How do I change the app language?

A: The app automatically detects your device's language setting. To change the language, modify your device's language settings in iOS/Android system preferences.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?

A: If you've previously purchased a subscription, you can restore it by going to Settings in the app and tapping "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.
